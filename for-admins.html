Admin Toolkit

Manage your team with clarity, not guesswork.

📈

Real-Time Dashboard

See live activity across your entire team. Calls being made right now, deals moving through stages, callbacks due, and docs outstanding — all updating in real time without refreshing.

👥

Rep Performance Metrics

Track calls made, conversations had, deals moved, and revenue generated per rep. Compare performance across the team. Identify top performers and reps who need coaching — backed by real data.

📥

Lead Distribution

Upload leads via CSV and distribute them to reps by campaign, territory, or round-robin. Control who gets what leads and how many. Redistribute leads when reps are out or overloaded.

🛠

Pipeline Visibility

See every deal in the pipeline by stage — from new lead through funded. Filter by rep, campaign, date range, or status. Know exactly where your revenue is and where deals are getting stuck.

🎯

Campaign Management

Create campaigns, assign lead lists, and track results by source. See which campaigns produce the most conversations and which convert to funded deals. Double down on what works.

📦

Export & Reporting

Export any report, any view, any time. Pull call logs, pipeline snapshots, rep scorecards, or campaign results into CSV for your own analysis or for sharing with partners and funders.
