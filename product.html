How It Works

From lead upload to funded deal. One workflow.

1. Upload & Distribute Leads

Import leads via CSV or connect your lead sources. Admins distribute leads to reps by campaign, territory, or round-robin. Every lead starts with a clean record.

📥
CSV → Campaign → Rep Queue

2. Call, Disposition & Move Forward

Reps work their queue. Each call gets a disposition — interested, callback, not interested, voicemail. Interested leads move to the next stage. The dialer loads the next lead automatically.

☎
Call → Disposition → Auto-Next

3. Collect Docs & Submit Deals

Request documents with one click. Track what's been received. When the file is complete, submit it to your funder — all without leaving the platform.

📄
Request → Receive → Submit

4. Track, Report & Grow

Every action is tracked. Managers see real-time dashboards with rep activity, pipeline value, and conversion rates. Use the data to coach reps and scale what works.

📊
Dashboards → Insights → Growth
