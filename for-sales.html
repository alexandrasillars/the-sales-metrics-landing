Auto-Next Dialing

After you disposition a call, the next lead loads automatically. You stay in the flow. No clicking back to a list, no finding the next number. Just call after call after call.

Your Workflow

A rep's day in The Sales Metrics.

1

Open Your Queue

Log in and your call list is ready. Fresh leads, scheduled callbacks, and follow-ups are all queued up by priority.

2

Dial & Disposition

Click to call. Talk to the merchant. Select a disposition. The next lead loads instantly. Stay in the zone.

3

Request & Collect Docs

Interested merchant? One click sends the doc request. Track what's received and what's outstanding — right on the deal record.

4

Move Deals Forward

Every action updates the pipeline. Docs received? Submit the deal. Approval comes in? Move to funded. Your pipeline always reflects reality.

9 AM

Start Dialing

Open your queue. 85 leads ready to call. Auto-next kicks in — you're through 20 calls in the first hour. Three callbacks scheduled.

10 AM

First Interest

Merchant wants to see numbers. One click sends the doc request email. You're already dialing the next lead before they even open it.

1 PM

Callbacks Pop Up

Three callbacks from this morning are due. They appear at the top of your queue. You connect with two — one is ready to submit docs today.

4 PM

Deals Moving

Bank statements came in for two files. You mark docs received, prep the submissions, and move both deals to "submitted." Your pipeline is clean and current.
